About Child Care Council of Nassau

As a Child Care Aware of America Quality Assured leading resource on child care and early learning experiences in Nassau County, the Council informs families about child care choices, indicators of quality and financial options. We support the child care workforce through programmatic enhancements (education, health, safety and nutrition) and business development. We also advocate for public policy changes that support families, the growth and development of children and the sustainability of the child care workforce. Further, we are proud to be partnered with community leaders, employers, public agencies and organizations to efficiently respond to and further develop the child care system.
